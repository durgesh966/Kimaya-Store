Kimaaya Kitchen King

Kimaaya Kitchen King presents "Hing (Asafoetida)," a product synonymous with absolute purity, natural essence, and unparalleled quality.
Our commitment at Kimaaya Kitchen King Hing is to deliver the ideal product to consumers at the right price. Before the inception of Kimaaya Kitchen King Hing, our dedicated team extensively researched the market and observed a prevalent trend of Hing compounds flooding the market. In response, we made a deliberate choice to provide Hing in its solid form, ensuring a unique and authentic offering for our discerning customers.

Our Product

Introducing Kimaaya Kitchen King Hing, offering a 10-gram jar of Asafoetida featuring Tazaki Hing Dana cultivated in the dry, cold regions surrounding the majestic areas of Afghanistan. Directly imported from Afghanistan, this premium Hing is available in two distinct varieties: the wet variant, known as Tazaki Shira, and the dry variant, referred to as Tazaki Hing Dana. Among these, Tazaki Hing Dana emerges as the superior choice. Kimaaya Kitchen King Hing guarantees unparalleled authenticity with its Tazaki Hing Dana, boasting a remarkable zero percent tincture content. This Hing is genuinely 100% original, meticulously sourced, and crafted to perfection.
